Tulsa Drywall Repair

Drywall Repair in Tulsa That Blends In Cleanly

If you have visible wall or ceiling damage, we repair it correctly and finish it cleanly so it does not stand out more than the original problem.

Contact Us Call or Text for a Quote

Good Fit For

  • • Holes in drywall, from minor dings to larger cutouts
  • • Water-damaged drywall and stained or softened areas
  • • Ceiling damage, including crack lines and patch zones
  • • Bad previous patches that still show through paint
  • • Texture mismatch and blending problems after prior repairs
  • • Drywall cracks tied to seam movement or settling

Repair Scope We Handle

  • • Drywall patching and repair
  • • Texture matching
  • • Ceiling drywall repair
  • • Repair after water damage
  • • Prep for paint-ready finish

How We Approach the Work

  • • 1) Assess the damage and cause: We inspect the visible issue and check for moisture, movement, or repeat-failure risk.
  • • 2) Repair and prep properly: We patch, stabilize, and prep correctly so repairs are not just surface-level fixes.
  • • 3) Match texture and finish: We blend texture and finish work so the repaired area fits the surrounding wall or ceiling.
