Structural and Water Management Solutions

If something in your home feels off: cracks, sagging, doors shifting, water where it should not be: precision matters. Structural and drainage issues require assessment before corrective work begins.

Structural Fixes

We solve the hardest home structural issues in Tulsa, from framing stabilization to garage correction.

Key Benefits

  • • Integrity: Prevent long-term damage to finishes and secondary systems.
  • • Security: Restore confidence in your home's stability and safety.
  • • Value: Protect your investment with documented, professional corrections.

Project Examples

Historic Framing Correction: Retrofitted modern reinforcement to 1920s floor joists to stop sagging and prepare for new finishes.

Water Management

Comprehensive drainage planning and installation to keep water away from your foundation.

Key Benefits

  • • Prevention: Stop water from reaching your crawlspace or foundation.
  • • Control: Engineered drainage paths (French drains) to handle heavy rain events.
  • • Sustainability: Protect siding, walkways, and landscaping from erosion.

Project Examples

Foundation Drainage: Installed a multi-zone grading and drainage system for a mid-century home experiencing water intrusion after heavy rain.
