Scope

Soffit, fascia, siding, and trim

We repair and replace damaged exterior components where it makes sense for the condition of the home—loose siding courses, cracked or water-stained soffit material, fascia boards pulling away, and trim that no longer sheds water correctly.

Exterior painting should follow sound substrate work. If peeling paint is telling you a deeper moisture story, we help map what to fix first so new paint does not lock in the wrong problem.

Water movement

Drainage-aware sequencing

When runoff, splash-back, or chronic moisture affects siding and trim, exterior repairs work best alongside a clear plan for how water should leave the area. For larger grading or drainage questions, we coordinate with structural and water-management support so repairs stay in the right order.

Example: drainage correction work that helps protect foundations and exterior finishes after the water path is addressed.
